Qiyi Kolors

Qiyi kolors is a game that arises from an oriental heritage and is inspired by a Japanese puzzle that tries to order numbers in ascending order and which is limited by a matrix of different dimensions.

After game starts click the squares to solve the gradient Qiyi

Click the center of the square to move it to the free space. You can pass the mouse on the image above to take a hint
